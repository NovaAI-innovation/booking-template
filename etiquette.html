Companion Etiquette

Mutual respect and consideration create the foundation for exceptional experiences. Please review these guidelines to ensure our time together is seamless and memorable.

Booking & Communication

  • Book in advance whenever possible. Last-minute requests may not always be accommodated.
  • Provide complete screening information promptly and honestly. This ensures both our safety and privacy.
  • Be clear and specific about date details including time, duration, and location preferences.
  • Communicate professionally and respectfully in all correspondence.
  • If plans change, provide as much notice as possible. Cancellation policies apply.

Hygiene & Presentation

  • Arrive freshly showered and well-groomed. Personal hygiene is non-negotiable.
  • Dress appropriately for the occasion. When in doubt, business casual or smart casual is recommended.
  • Use breath mints or gum before our meeting. Avoid heavy cologne or strong scents.
  • Maintain good oral hygiene including fresh breath and clean teeth.
  • Keep nails clean and trimmed for comfort and safety.

Punctuality & Time Management

  • Arrive on time. Punctuality demonstrates respect for my schedule.
  • If running late, communicate immediately. Extended delays may result in shortened sessions.
  • Respect agreed-upon time limits. Extensions require advance approval and additional compensation.
  • Do not expect overtime or rush preparations due to your tardiness.

Financial Etiquette

  • Present the agreed-upon donation at the beginning of our date in an unsealed envelope.
  • Have the exact amount in clean, crisp bills. Do not expect change to be available.
  • Never discuss, negotiate, or question rates during our appointment.
  • Deposits are non-refundable except in cases of emergency on my part.
  • Additional expenses (dinner, drinks, entertainment, travel) are separate from my companionship fee.

Boundaries & Respect

  • Respect all stated boundaries and limitations without exception.
  • Consent is ongoing and can be withdrawn at any time. "No" means no.
  • Do not attempt to negotiate services or activities I have explicitly declined.
  • Treat me with the same courtesy and respect you would expect in return.
  • Intoxication or substance use that impairs judgment is unacceptable and grounds for termination.

Privacy & Discretion

  • Maintain complete confidentiality about our arrangement and any personal details shared.
  • No photography, video, or audio recording unless explicitly agreed upon in advance.
  • Do not share my contact information or advertise our arrangement to others.
  • Be discreet when arriving and departing from meeting locations.
  • Respect my privacy by not attempting to discover personal information beyond what I choose to share.

During Our Time Together

  • Put your phone on silent and minimize distractions. Give me your full attention.
  • Be present and engaged. Quality conversation and connection are valued.
  • Follow my lead regarding the pace and progression of our date.
  • If attending public venues, be aware of social dynamics and behave appropriately.
  • Ask before touching or initiating physical contact. Consent applies throughout our time together.

General Courtesy

  • Arrive sober or with only moderate alcohol consumption. Excessive intoxication will result in appointment cancellation.
  • If meeting at a hotel, secure the room in your name and have it ready prior to my arrival.
  • Provide a clean, comfortable, and safe environment for our date.
  • Be gracious if I need to end our time together early due to boundary violations or discomfort.
  • Leave a positive review or testimonial if you enjoyed our time (on approved platforms only).

Thank you for respecting these guidelines. They ensure that our time together is comfortable, safe, and mutually enjoyable. Gentlemen who honor these standards are always welcomed back and prioritized for future bookings. I look forward to creating unforgettable memories together.
